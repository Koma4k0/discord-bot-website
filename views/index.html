Frequently Asked Questions

What hosting solution does Sound Wave use?

Sound Wave utilizes a robust, high-performance cloud hosting solution to ensure optimal uptime and responsiveness for our users. We're constantly evaluating our infrastructure to provide the best possible experience.

Does Sound Wave offer partnership opportunities?

While we're currently focused on enhancing our core services and growing our user base, we're always open to exploring mutually beneficial partnerships. Please contact Koma4k for partnership opportunities.

How can I support the development of Sound Wave?

The best way to support Sound Wave is by using the bot, providing feedback, and spreading the word. We're exploring various support options for the future, including premium features. Stay tuned for updates!

Is Sound Wave available 24/7?

Yes, Sound Wave is designed to operate 24/7. In the rare event of downtime, we work quickly to resolve any issues.

How can I get support for Sound Wave?

For immediate assistance, you can use the /help command within Discord. For more complex issues, contact Koma4k via discord.

How can I offer suggestions for Sound Wave?

To offer suggestions for Sound Wave you can use our /suggestions command to send suggestions directly to Koma4k
